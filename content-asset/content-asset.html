📦 SFCC Content Assets Translator (AI Tools)

We are delighted to announce that Content Assets Translator is now available in the Business Manager.

Content Assets are widely used across all SFCC sites for editorial, marketing, and technical content.

The Content Asset Translator is a feature available under AI Tools > Content Asset Translator that allows getting the translation of the Default locale of a given content asset to another locale using AI. That avoid human mistakes or typos that can happen especially when html markup is mixed up with regular texts.

Users can decide to save that translation to the locale of the given content asset. It is also possible for the users to make some adjustments before saving the translation.

🧰 Content Assets Key Concepts & Best Practices

All content assets are managed in Business Manager: Merchant Tools > Content > Content Assets.

A Content Asset set to "Online" is publicly accessible via:

https://yourwebsite.com/content-id.html

👉 Even if it is no longer used anywhere on the site.


Business Manager > Content Assets
Example

🔎 Real Life Example

A past promotion page (-30% campaign) was created as a Content Asset and set to online.

Months later:

  • The page was no longer linked on the website
  • But it was still accessible via URL
  • And had been indexed by Google

👉 Result: customers accessed outdated offers and customer service received complaints.

⚠️ Key Risks

  • Unused content still publicly accessible
  • Old promotions still visible
  • SEO indexing of unintended pages
  • Legal or compliance issues

📊 Did you know?
Search engines can discover and index content assets even if they are not linked internally.

🧠 Best Practices

  • Set unused content assets to Offline
  • Audit content regularly
  • Avoid duplication
  • Define ownership
  • Use clear naming conventions

🖐️ Key Actions

  1. Review content assets regularly
  2. Identify unused ones
  3. Set them to Offline
  4. Clean up obsolete content

👉 Content Assets are not just content blocks — they are public pages.

Proper management is key to ensuring SEO performance, compliance, and a consistent customer experience.
